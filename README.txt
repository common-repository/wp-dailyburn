== Description ==

A DailyBurn widget for your WordPress blog.

[DailyBurn](http://dailyburn.com/) is a great website if you're trying to lose weight or get in shape. You can count calories, track your weight loss and exercise progress, and find other like-minded healthy folks to discuss health related topics, and offer encouragement to each other.

The WP-DailyBurn widget displays your DailyBurn profile stats in your blog's sidebar.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `wp-dailyburn` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin from the Plugins menu.
1. Add the WP-DailyBurn widget to you sidebar from the Appearance -> Widgets menu.
1. Enter your DailyBurn username & password into the widget's options menu.
